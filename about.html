About BFB-X

Business Fest Bangladesh 2026 – Edition X, The Golden Edition, celebrates decades of excellence, leadership and innovation.

Business Fest Bangladesh (BFB)

Business Fest Bangladesh is the flagship national-level business festival organized by Notre Dame Business Club (NDBC). Over the years, BFB has evolved into one of the most prestigious student-driven platforms in the country, bringing together young talents from schools and colleges nationwide.

BFB-X marks the Golden Edition of this journey — bigger in scale, sharper in competition and richer in experience.

Notre Dame Business Club (NDBC)

Established in 1973, Notre Dame Business Club is one of the oldest and most respected college-level clubs in Bangladesh. For over 50 years, NDBC has been dedicated to nurturing leadership, analytical thinking, creativity and professionalism among students.

Through initiatives like Business Fest Bangladesh, NDBC continues to empower future leaders by creating platforms that challenge minds and refine skills beyond textbooks.

Our Vision & Mission

The vision of BFB-X is to create an inclusive and competitive environment where students can explore business, creativity and problem-solving at a national and international level.

Our mission is to inspire young minds, foster ethical leadership, encourage innovation and prepare participants for real-world challenges.

Notre Dame Business Club (NDBC) has been creating platforms for decades where ideas are challenged, skills are refined and future leaders emerge.
