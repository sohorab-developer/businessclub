Business Fest Bangladesh 2026 - The Golden Edition

This edition brings together a diverse range of competitive, creative and knowledge-based segments, designed to ensure that every participant finds a platform to showcase their true potential. With renewed vision and elevated standards, BFB-X is set to deliver an experience that is larger, sharper and more impactful than ever before.

From the traditional Biz Olympiad to innovative segments like Corporate Senate and E-Sports, BFB-X offers something for every aspiring business leader, creative thinker, and problem solver. The festival is meticulously designed to challenge participants, foster teamwork, and develop real-world skills that extend beyond the classroom.

Why Participate in BFB-X?

BFB-X is not just another competition; it's a transformative experience that prepares young minds for the challenges of the business world. Participants gain exposure to industry experts, develop critical thinking skills, and build networks that last a lifetime.

Notre Dame Business Club (NDBC) has been creating platforms for decades where ideas are challenged, skills are refined and future leaders emerge.
